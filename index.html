Contact Me


With a bachelor's degree in hand, from Temple University, I embarked on a journey of creativity and expression through filmmaking. Over the years I have amassed experience as a Director, Cinematographer, Producer, Editor, Gaffer, Grip, AD, Production Manager, and PA. Extensive and varied, I know, but I don't shy away from learning something new. My work has encompassed Music Videos, Documentaries, Feature Films, Instructional Videos, Short Films, Spec Commercials, and TV pilots. Over time I got into photography as a hobby to keep "my eye busy" and discover new ways of seeing.

As we are now fully engrossed in this age of information, I find myself returning to a seed planted in me long ago before the internet, the days of the Commodore 64... Programming or as it is now termed... Coding. A familiar place from whence I came.. sitting at home in front of a computer screen trying to figure some "ish" out.

With Old tools and new tools (or old tools rediscovered), I present to the world... undrstatd... my vehicle for creation, collaboration, production, and self-expression.
